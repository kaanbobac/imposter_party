# Privacy Policy for BoomeR

**Effective Date:** January 2, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 2, 2025

## Overview

BoomeR ("Kim Bu Aramızdaki Boomer?") is a social deduction party game developed by Kaan Bobac. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Player Names**: Names entered by players during gameplay sessions
- **Game Preferences**: Settings such as game duration, number of players, and category selections

### Information We Don't Collect
- Personal identification information
- Contact information (email, phone numbers)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Social media profiles
- Payment information

## How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for:
- **Gameplay Functionality**: Displaying player names during game sessions
- **Game Settings**: Remembering your preferred game configurations
- **Local Storage Only**: All data remains on your device

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: All game data is stored locally on your device only
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store any information on external servers
- **Session-Based**: Player names and game data are temporary and reset when you start a new game
- **No Data Transmission**: Your information is never transmitted to our servers or third parties

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties. Since all data remains local to your device, there is no data sharing whatsoever.

## Third-Party Services

BoomeR does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

BoomeR is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since our app only collects temporary gameplay information (player names) that remains on the device, no personal data of children is stored or transmitted.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:
- **Access**: All your data is visible within the app
- **Deletion**: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
- **Control**: You can clear game data by resetting the app or starting new games

## International Users

BoomeR is designed for Turkish-speaking users but is available globally. All data processing occurs locally on your device, regardless of your location.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since the app doesn't collect personal data, policy changes will primarily relate to clarifications or new features.
